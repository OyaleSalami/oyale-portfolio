Back to Portfolio

Project Overview

Santa was kidnapped by rogue Snowmen, so Fritz the elf is off to the rescue! Solve puzzles and explore an ice cave while battling evil toys in this nostalgic 16-bit adventure game.

• Fight a variety of toys! • Strike with both melee and ranged weapons! • Explore a maze-like ice cave! • Relive the glory of classic 16-bit games!

Gameplay Mechanics

Players control Fritz the elf, navigating through levels filled with puzzles and enemies. Use melee attacks for close combat and ranged weapons for distance. The game features retro pixel art and sound design reminiscent of classic 16-bit era games.

Technologies Used

Developed using web technologies for browser-based play. Includes HTML5 canvas for rendering, JavaScript for game logic, and possibly a game engine like Phaser.

Development Story

Created for the Christian Game Developers Conference in 2024. The theme revolves around holiday rescue and overcoming obstacles, with a focus on fun, family-friendly gameplay.
