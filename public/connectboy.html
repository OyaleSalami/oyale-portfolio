Project Overview

ConnectBoy Games is a mobile gaming platform dedicated to bringing players together through innovative, engaging multiplayer experiences. Founded with a passion for gaming and social connection, we develop high-quality games that prioritize user satisfaction, security, and fair play.

Our mission is to create a welcoming gaming community where players of all levels can enjoy competitive and casual gaming experiences. The platform features real-time multiplayer games across iOS and Android devices, with seamless cross-platform play.

Games Included

  • Elite Archery: Test your precision and timing in realistic archery challenges with wind effects.
  • Connect Four: Classic strategy game. Align four discs before your opponent to win.
  • Dots and Boxes: Strategic pencil-and-paper game. Claim more territory by completing boxes.
  • Mini Golf 3D: Master challenging courses with precision putting and creative shots.
  • Compact Soccer: Fast-paced sports action on a mini pitch for ultimate competition.
  • Tic Tac Toe: The timeless classic reimagined for quick and fun multiplayer matches.

Technologies Used

Built using Unity for cross-platform development, with backend services for multiplayer functionality. Features secure data encryption and social features like chat and clans.

Challenges & Learnings

Developing for multiple platforms required careful optimization. Implementing real-time multiplayer involved networking challenges and ensuring fair play.
