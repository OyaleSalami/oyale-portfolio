Project Overview

In this VR tower defense game, protect your community of survivors from the corrupting forces of Malice. Harness your gravity glove to collect and sling scraps, construct turrets, and guard the builders as they repair the wall.

Wield your gravity glove to fight, build, and scan enemies. Battle five different enemy types with unique attacks, from small and slimy Bitterness to the heavily-armored tank, Malice—all inspired by Ephesians 4.

Gameplay Features

  • Fight: Capture enemies in your gravity beam and launch to send them packing!
  • Build: Gather scrap metal to build turrets and fortify your defenses.
  • Scan: Uncover the strengths and weaknesses of each enemy type and learn how best to defeat them.

Technologies Used

Developed for VR using Unity, targeting Meta Quest 2 and 3 headsets. Includes physics-based interactions and procedural enemy AI.

Development Process

Created as part of the Christian Game Developers Conference, focusing on themes of protection and community. Involved VR-specific design challenges and optimization for standalone VR hardware.
